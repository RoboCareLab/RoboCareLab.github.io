RBE 595: Social AI and Robotics

The course covers socially assistive robotics, social HRI, human-centered computing (LLMs, affective computing, and personalization) etc. The course is designed for graduate students and senior undergraduates.

RBE 3100 - Social Implications Of Robotics (C-term)

This course introduces students to the social, moral, ethical, legal, and current or future philosophical issues within the context of robotic systems and related emerging technology.
