Welcome to the Socially Intelligent Robotics for Healthcare (RoboCare) Lab
Our Research
We focus on developing socially intelligent robots that can assist in healthcare settings. Our research combines artificial intelligence, robotics engineering, and human-computer interaction to create systems that understand and respond to human needs.
Our Projects
Our ongoing projects include assistive robots for elderly care, rehabilitation robotics, and emotionally responsive companion robots. We are committed to creating technology that enhances human wellbeing and quality of life.
